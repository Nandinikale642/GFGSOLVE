About This Page

This webpage introduces an AI-powered chatbot designed to assist with coding-related queries. You can ask any coding-related question, and the chatbot will provide an answer based on its trained models. If you're not satisfied with the response, you can click on a "Not Satisfied" button (represented as an 'X' icon). This will generate a unique ID for the query and transfer it to a platform where coding experts can review it. Once reviewed, you can use the unique ID to retrieve the expert-approved answer.
